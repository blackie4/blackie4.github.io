Sublime Text 安装sass语法高亮

  1. ctrl+shift+p (Win, Linux) or cmd+shift+p (OS X) 打开Package Control，搜索sass，点击安装
  2. 关闭sublime，重新打开.scss文件
  3. Menu > View > Syntax > Open all with current extension as… > Sass
